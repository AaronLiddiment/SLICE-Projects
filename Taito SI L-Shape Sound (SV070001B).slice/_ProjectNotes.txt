When testing the shifter circuit (ICs 2, 3, 5, 6, 9, 10, 14 & 17) ensure that you have a game in progress or the ICs will not be active.

Caution: This board has a 12v supply for the sound section. Ensure that the test clip and ground do not come into contact with voltages over 5v or damage to the Digilent unit may occur.
